Japan isn't all about anime and samurai, it's culture and art is one of a kind

About Art

Japanese art covers a wide range of art styles and media, including ancient pottery, sculpture in wood and bronze, ink painting on silk and paper, calligraphy, ceramics, architecture, oil painting, literature, drama and music

About anime

Anime is a style of hand-drawn and computer-generated animation originating from Japan. It often draws on history, folklore, mythology, literature and other traditional sources, while some of the most popular anime shows around the world fall into the fantasy, adventure and science fiction genres.

JPNDrawing

There are many drawing websites available online. Some popular ones include Sketchpad which allows you to create digital artwork and share it online, Kleki which is a free web-app for painting and editing with features such as different brushes, layers, filters, and pressure sensitivity, and Pixilart which is a free online pixel art drawing tool
